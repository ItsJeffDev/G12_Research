You are tasked with building a system that manages different types of employees and calculates their monthly salary. There are: Full-time employees with a fixed monthly salary. Part-time employees paid by the hour. The system should: Store employee information. Calculate salary based on employee type. Display a payroll report.

Total Employees: 3
